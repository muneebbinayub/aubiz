ABOUT US

Welcome to our exquisite guest house nestled in the heart of the breathtaking Kashmir Valley, specifically in Nishat, Srinagar. Situated on the serene shores of Dal Lake, our guest house offers an unparalleled experience of tranquility and natural beauty. Located a mere 150 meters away from the Boulevard fringe of Dal Lake and just a short 3-minute stroll from the historic Nishat Mughal Garden, our location is a true haven for nature enthusiasts and history lovers alike. Stepping inside, you'll find that our guest house interior exudes the charm of Kashmir's heritage with its traditional mud plaster and paneled finish. The building resonates with the aura of Kashmiri culture. Complementing this rich heritage is a sprawling garden spanning 3000 square meters, offering stunning panoramic views of the Zabarwan Hills and the picturesque Dal Lake. You'll have the opportunity to capture the beauty of Shankaracharya Temple and Hazratbal Shrine, both visible from the vicinity of the guest house. One of the most remarkable features of our guest house is its tranquil setting away from the main road and bustling traffic. Here, you'll find solace in the soothing sounds of nature, including the melodies of insects at night, providing an undisturbed and serene atmosphere. Experience the true essence of Nishat as you savor the peace and calm that our guest house provides. For those eager to explore the historical gems of the region, our guest house is conveniently located close to various royal historic sites. From Nishat Bagh and Shalimar Garden to Harwan Garden, Dachigam National Park, Pari Mahal, Tulip Garden, Char Chinar, Dal Lake, Zabarwan Hills, Shankaracharya Temple, Chashme Shahi Park, Raj Bhavan, and Makai Point, all these treasures are within easy reach, promising an enriching and memorable stay. And there's more! Embark on a thrilling adventure as the path to Mahadev Mountain, perfect for trekking, the road is right from our guest house. Experience the exhilaration of trekking through the picturesque landscapes of the Kashmir Valley while enjoying the convenience of starting your journey from our doorstep. Come and immerse yourself in the lap of nature's beauty, history, tranquility, and adventure. Experience the enchantment of the Kashmir Valley through our guest house and create memories that will last a lifetime.
